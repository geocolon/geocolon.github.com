ABOUT ME

Born and raised in Brooklyn by my Dominican and Puerto Rican mother, my father is Jamaican and Chinese. My first memories were watching a Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles cartoon after school at 4 years old. Ever since then I’ve loved cartoons. From cartoons I started drawing other cartoons, this gave me an interest in art, illustration, and comic books and sequential art.

At around age 13 I started taking fine art classes at the Brooklyn Museum. Every Saturday morning I would take these classes and it help me appreciate fine art and it’s history.

I F*@KING LOVE MOVIES!

Quoting movies is my favorite thing to do with friends. If you are able to catch any quotes that I’ve dropped from The Big Lebowski, then rest assured you are a good friend of mine. For me movies are like social currency, much like what sports is to most people.
Movies tell me who you are and what you are interested in. In my opinion there’s a movie out there for everyone.
