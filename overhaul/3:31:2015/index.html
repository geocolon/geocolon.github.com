ABOUT ME

From Brooklyn, I was born and raised by a single mother of Puerto Rican & Dominican Descent. My first memories when I was a child was of Saturday morning cartoons. “Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles” The cartoons I watched gave me a passion for drawing and painting. With supported to keep drawing the cartoons that I loved I became better at illustrating. As a teenager I discovered Japanese anime, my favorite anime at the time was “Tecknoman”, it was a saga with a serious story line. This is where I learn that cartoons were a medium that was not defined just for little kids but could be used as for a more complex narratives.

The first two years of college I majored in fine arts. This is where I learn about art history and painting techniques. The last two years I changed my major to digital design and graphic design. Those last two years of college I learn to love technology, it was hard not too when computers were becoming exponentially faster and the iPhone was changing the world. I remember “Facebook” was “The Facebook” and I needed a college email to start an account while the peak of social media at the time was MySpace and Friendster. Now I could not see my life without a smartphone in my hand.

• • •

Now I work for Penguin Random House as a Digital Production Associate. My job is simply turning books into ebooks for “Kindle” and “iPad”.
